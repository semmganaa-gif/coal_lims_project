- **SURFACE_SAMPLE_NAMES:** 5 дээж (Ламинар бокс x 2, Зейтца 1-3)

  ```sql
  ALTER TABLE sample ADD CONSTRAINT ck_sample_client_name CHECK (

    'naimdain','maiga','tsagaan_khad','sum','uurhaichin','tsetsii',
    'gallerey','negdsen_office','uutsb','sbutsb','busad','dotood_khyanalt')
  );
  ```
